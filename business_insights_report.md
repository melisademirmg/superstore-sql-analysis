\# Business Insights Report



\## Project Overview



This project analyzes retail sales data from the Superstore dataset using SQL in Google BigQuery.



The objective is to identify sales trends, customer behavior, product performance, and profitability insights.



\---



\## Key Findings



\### Category Performance



Technology generated the highest sales revenue and the highest total profit among all categories.



Technology Profit Margin: 17.40%



Office Supplies Profit Margin: 17.04%



Furniture Profit Margin: 2.49%



\---



\### Product Performance



Highest Single Sale:



Cisco TelePresence System EX90 Videoconferencing Unit



Sale Amount: $22,638.48



Most Profitable Product:



Canon imageCLASS 2200 Advanced Copier



Total Profit: $25,199.93



\---



\### Customer Analysis



Highest Spending Customer:



Sean Miller



Total Sales: $25,043.05



Most Profitable Customer:



Tamara Chand



Total Profit: $8,981.32



\---



\## Business Recommendations



1\. Increase focus on high-performing Technology products.



2\. Investigate low profit margins within the Furniture category.



3\. Develop loyalty strategies for high-value customers.



4\. Expand promotion of high-profit copier products.
